Chapo

Recipe Credit: Jane Mburu

Description

A fluffy tender flatbread originating from India but traveling all the way to East Africa where I first enjoyed it. Goes great with soups, legumes, and hot beverages.

Ingredients

  • Wheat Flour
  • Cooking Oil
  • Milk
  • Sugar
  • Salt

Instructions

Prepwork

  1. Warm a cup of milk or water
  2. Mix with egg/s, salt, sugar, and oil
  3. Then stir while adding flour until you can't stir anymore
  4. Knead the dough until it stops sticking to your hands
  5. Add a bit of oil and continue kneading
  6. Cover the dough and let it rest for 1+ hour

Cooking

  1. Divide dough into balls about the size of cupping your hand
  2. Roll each ball, apply oil and spread it, then fold it up like a cinnamon roll
  3. Roll each dough again and spread it
  4. Take the dough spread out into a big circle and place them on the pan
  5. Put oil into pan and put the dough or put dough and flip it over and put oil while cooking
  6. Cook on medium heat until golden brown
  7. Enjoy!
